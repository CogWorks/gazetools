Gaze data from a SMI RED 500 eyetracker at 500 Hz.

Usage

data(smi)

Format

  • smi_time: sample time
  • smi_type: sample type
  • smi_sxl: horizontal gaze location of left eye
  • smi_sxr: horizontal gaze location of right eye
  • smi_syl: vertical gaze location of left eye
  • smi_syr: vertical gaze location of right eye
  • smi_dxl: width of left pupil
  • smi_dxr: width of right pupil
  • smi_dyl: height of left pupil
  • smi_dyr: height of right pupil
  • smi_exl: horizontal offset of the left eye relative to the center of the screen
  • smi_exr: horizontal offset of the right eye relative to the center of the screen
  • smi_eyl: vertical offset of the left eye relative to the center of the screen
  • smi_eyr: vertical offset of the right eye relative to the center of the screen
  • smi_ezl: distance of the left eye to the center of the screen
  • smi_ezr: distance of the right eye to the center of the screen

Description

Gaze data of a participant performing a visual search task.

Details

  • Samplerate was 500hz.
  • Screen resolution was 1680 x 1050 (px).
  • Screen size was 473.76 x 296.1 (mm).
