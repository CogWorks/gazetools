Gaze data from a SMI RED 500 eyetracker at 500 Hz.

Usage

data(smi)

Format

smi_timesample time smi_typesample type smi_sxlhorizontal gaze location of left eye smi_sxrhorizontal gaze location of right eye smi_sylvertical gaze location of left eye smi_syrvertical gaze location of right eye smi_dxlwidth of left pupil smi_dxrwidth of right pupil smi_dylheight of left pupil smi_dyrheight of right pupil smi_exlhorizontal offset of the left eye relative to the center of the screen smi_exrhorizontal offset of the right eye relative to the center of the screen smi_eylvertical offset of the left eye relative to the center of the screen smi_eyrvertical offset of the right eye relative to the center of the screen smi_ezldistance of the left eye to the center of the screen smi_ezrdistance of the right eye to the center of the screen

Description

Gaze data of a participant performing a visual search task.

Details

  • Samplerate was 500hz.
  • Screen resolution was 1680 x 1050 (px).
  • Screen size was 473.76 x 296.1 (mm).
